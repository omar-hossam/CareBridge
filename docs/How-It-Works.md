# How It Works ❔

I'm going to explain how does the platform works in an easy way using scenarios so you can see the full picture and the core idea.

---

## Scenario 🇦

Person X is sick so he's going to the hospital but wait.. he doesn't have money to pay the bill or the medicine.. he is lucky because the hospital is verified on our platform so the hospital treats him for free at no cost. Then the hospital sends a **donation request on our platform**. The bill + medicine cost $100 and includes:

1. Medicine names 💊
2. Quantities e.g. 3
3. Costs 💲
4. Hospital name + stamp on the bill image 🏥
5. Patient fake ID (generated by our platform) #

**NOT** patient's photo, name, national ID, phone number, etc. Why? Because we respect patients' privacy.

Now the donation request is public on the `/requests` page so donors can donate to the hospital safely.

### 🪜 The Flow Summary

1. Sick patient ➡️ Hospital (verified) ➡️ Treats for free 🆓 ➡️ Uploads bill (no patient name/face)️
2. Donation request public 🌐
3. Donor sees bill photo 🧾
4. Donor pays hospital $100 😌
5. Hospital gets money 😊
6. Patient already healthy ✅

> **No money? No problem. No privacy violation? Guaranteed.**

## Scenario 🇧

Person Y needs medicine. He's standing outside the pharmacy but wait.. he has no money to buy it. He knows the medicine name, he knows how much it costs, but his wallet is empty 😢

He doesn't have a bill because he just ran out of medicine. Or maybe he lost it. Or maybe he never had one.

He is lucky because our platform has a feature called **"Individual Request"** 👤

He opens the platform, clicks the button, and enters:

1. Medicine name 💊
2. Cost 💲
3. His country 🌍
4. City 🏙️
5. Pharmacy location on Google Maps 📍 
6. Pharmacy name Ⓜ️
7. His WhatsApp or Telegram (so donors can contact him directly if they want) 📱
8. Uploads the bill (includes official stamp BUT not his personal info for his privacy!) 🧾
9. Urgent 🚨
10. Then he **swears** the request is true (checkbox includes a legal warning — fake requests = police involved) 👮‍♂️🛡️

*Blur your name and face. Keep medicine names + stamp visible.*

The request goes public on `/requests` page 🌐

A donor sees it. The donor talks with him on WhatsApp (Outside the platform!), donor has the full right to ask him about the bill and so on until he is relaxed to pay.

Person Y sends the bill photo 🧾

The donor is relaxed now. He sees the medicine name matches, the price matches, the pharmacy stamp is real ✅

The donor pays Person Y directly 💲

Person Y buys the medicine and gets healthy 💊

### 🪜 The Flow Summary

1. Person needs medicine ➡️ Clicks "Individual Request" ➡️ Creates account <5min, fill the reqeust form
2. Request public 🌐
3. Donor messages on WhatsApp/Telegram (if still unsure) 💬
4. Person sends bill photo 🧾
5. Donor pays person 💲
6. Person buys medicine ✅

> **No bill? No problem. Donor decides. Fake request? Police involved.**

## Scenario 🇨

Person Z was sick and couldn't afford the hospital bill, but this hospital is **not registered with us**... Is it a problem? Is he unlucky?

**NO! We are ready for ALL scenarios! ✅**

So what should he do now? Simple:

He opens the platform from his phone, clicks **"Individual Request"**, and fills the same form as Scenario B — but replaces **Pharmacy Name** with **Hospital Name**. That's it!

> ⚠️ He should tell the hospital: *"I'm asking for donation from CareBridge — we're going to be recognized globally, so please wait for me."*

He blurs his name and face from the bill. Only medicine names, costs, and hospital stamp remain visible.

Then donors see the request, verify, and pay.

**Unregistered hospital? No problem.**

---

## 🏥🏧 What About Hospital Verification?

1. Hospital applies on platform via `/signup-hospital`
2. Hospital **has to fill strict form fields and upload legal documents verified by the government**
3. Admin verifies: license, physical address, stamp **IF NOT TRUSTED OR SOMETHING MISSING/WRONG -> HE CAN REFUSE IT**
4. Hospital gets **verified badge** ✅
5. Donors trust verified hospitals 

--- 

## 🔔 What About Notification System?

1. Donor X follows hospital Y 
2. Hospital Y requested a new donation 
3. Donor X gets a notification in `/notifications` 
4. Donor X donates to the hospital 😊

**Note:** for donors' privacy, No one can see the profile's donor or info except his name, country and donated money are seen by the hospital only.

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Does CareBridge store patient photos?**  
A: **Never.** Only bills with medicine names + cost.

**Q: What if a patient has no bill?**  
A: Use **"HELP! Medicine in need"** feature (Scenario 2).

**Q: Is this legal?**  
A: Yes. No real patient identifiers = no PHI under HIPAA.

**Q: Who verifies requests?**  
A: **Donors do.** Platform connects. Donor decides.

**Note:** `/legal` page for legal info ⚖️
